Welcome to Easy Succulents!

Succulents are my passion! I love growing them, propagating them, and most recently... writing about them. These plants are amazing and have more to them than people think!

Who I am?

Welcome! My name is Jimena and I'm the founder of Easy Succulents. When I was a little girl I remember that my older sister bought an Aloe Vera plant. For some reason I was so fascinated by that spiky little plant.

Ever since I've been a Succulent fanatic! I love growing Succulents (not just Aloe Vera!), I grow them, nurture them, propagate them, and most importantly for you... I love writing about Succulents!

I know how hard it is to find good information online about Succulents. So I decided to create a blog where I share my experience with all the types of Succulents that I'm currently growing and hopefully, be able to help as many people benefit from these awesome plants!

Join our Email List!

My most engaged readers come through my email list. And for good reason! I share special tips and info that I don't share anywhere else. You can unsubscribe at any time, but almost everybody does. We must be doing something right!

Learn With Me!

I've spent so much time learning how to grow Succulents the right way (the best soil, how much light they need, the best -and easiest- Succulent types depending on the weather, etc.) that I want to share everything I've learnt with the world.

This blog has all the information you could possibly need to start growing your Succulents like a pro. Don't make the mistakes I did! You'll learn first hand how to grow succulents the right way!

These are a couple of resources to get started in my blog. I hope you like them!
